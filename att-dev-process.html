Attigo

The Attigo site is one of my newest projects for Nelnet. Built completely from the ground up in a CMS called Hubspot, this project was completed quickly in just one months time. It was my first experience developing inside of a CMS. As the sole developer on this project (lead), it required a lot of diligence and focus to meet the deadline while dealing with the learning curve of a new technology.

What I did:

  • Built a fully responsive, multi-level website on a short timeline
  • Used HubL (hubspot language), HTML5, and CSS3 to create templates and reusable modules for the clients
  • Collaborated with designers to work through issues found in Hubspot
  • Attended and facilitated client reviews and stand-ups
