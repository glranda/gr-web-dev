About Me

Hi, I’m Grayson. I’m 25 years old and I’m a Senior Front-End Web Developer and Freelance UI/UX Designer. Welcome to my Portfolio! Everything under the “Design” section above was designed and also coded entirely by me (including this portfolio), and everything under the “Development” section was coded by me. Download my resume

In 2015 I graduated from Illinois State University with a Bachelors in Arts Technology. Afterwards, I moved to Madison, WI where I currently work and live. I love the feeling of a friendly and clear interface, no matter the user or device, therefore my focus is on Responsiveness, Accessibility, and Usability.

I also have a passion for live music, world culture, and cooking. I try to fill up my life with exciting adventures and of course, great food. If you would like to know more, share an interest with me, or just want to say hello - feel free to contact me.
