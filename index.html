About Me

In 2015 I graduated from Illinois State University with a Bachelors in Arts Technology. Afterwards, I moved to New York City to become a Front-End Engineer and have since broadened my interest to Freelance UI/UX Design. I love exploring new technologies and writing lean, semantic code for all types of interfaces; meant for any user or device. That is why my focus is on Responsiveness, Accessibility, and User Experience.
