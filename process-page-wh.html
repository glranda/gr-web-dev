Wyldhaven

Wyldhaven is a music producer based out of Madison, Wisconsin. I was asked to assist in branding, design, photography, and coding for the website. The goal was to deliver a cohesive site and brand that could mentally stick with new listeners. After finding some initial success, I was eventually tasked with designing the print media as well.
