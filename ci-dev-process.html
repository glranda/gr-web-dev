Community Investments

The Community Investments website is the flagship site of the philanthropic branch of Nelnet. It serves as a platform to announce grant recipients and information for schools and borrowers. This is a large multi-level website that required a long development timeline with a dedicated development team. This was one of my first projects, and was completed using the Agile methodology.

What I did:

  • Site architecture and mapping in the early stages of development
  • Built global and reusable styles in CSS3 and LESS
  • Wrote semantic HTML5 to comply with the rules of Accessibility
  • Assisted with Client to Development team relations and negotiations
  • Other languages used: jQuery, JavaScript, JSON
